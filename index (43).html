Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

GulfVisa Pro ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the personal data of your clients and your agency. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data on our platform.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data to provide our services:

  • Agency data: Name, email, license number, phone, address
  • Client data: Full name, date of birth, passport details, religion, marital status, photo, employer information
  • Usage data: Login times, CV generation history, plan status

2. Why We Collect It

Client data is collected solely to generate official visa CV documents on behalf of licensed visa agencies. We do not sell, share, or use this data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. How We Store and Protect It

All data is stored in Supabase-managed databases with row-level security — meaning each agency can only access their own client data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Client photos are stored in private, access-controlled storage buckets.

4. Data Retention

Client data is retained for as long as your agency account is active. You may delete any client record at any time from your dashboard. On account deletion, all associated client data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you or your clients are in the EU/EEA or jurisdictions with similar data protection laws, you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete personal data. Contact us at privacy@gulfvisapro.com to exercise these rights.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing (we do not store card details)
  • Twilio — WhatsApp notification delivery

7. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@gulfvisapro.com
